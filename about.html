How to play

First of all, you must have Minetest Engine installed on your device.

Options for smartphones or tablets are:

    Multicraft for Apple devices
    Minetest/Multicraft for Android
    On Linux OS you can get Minetest from their software manager or command line, but on Windows OS you can download it from the official page here.

Once you have Minetest or Multicraft depending on your case. the connection is very simple and intuitive.
    Search the list for "Moonlight Falls" or directly type the address: moonlight.edgy1.net and the port: 30028 in Minetest.
    To connect from Multicraft enter the following format: 148.251.42.186:30028 in the invitation code box
You must choose a username that will be used to connect to our server. Don't forget to include a secure password as well and not share it with anyone.
